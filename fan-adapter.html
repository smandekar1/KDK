    • Product Description


    KDK manufactures fan adapters in a variety of shapes and sizes. These components are also referred to as fan hubs. They are used in the automotive industry. KDK provides fan adapers in a machined finished condition. Raw forgings that can be finished machined are avaialble as well. These fan adapters can be packaged in customer containers or can be shipped in disposable packaging. Heat treating services as well as normalizing for ease of machining, quench and tempering for added hardness are also offered. We use the upset forging process which adds strength to the steel verses machining from solid stock and uses less material than machining from a solid bar. Improved grain flow which is achieved as a result of this process, provides additional strenght. Depending on your requirements, KDK will make a custom die for your forging needs. Please send us a copy of your drawing and we will be happy to review your part and send a quote to you.
