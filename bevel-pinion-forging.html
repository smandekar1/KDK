Bevel Pinion Forging

Bevel pinions provide a more near net forging shape requiring less finish machining

Features


  • Up to 4" dia. and various lengths
  • Can be supplied in a near net as forged condition
  • Forging steps designed per customer specification
  • Available in alloy steel materials


    • Product Description


    Bevel pinion forgings are used in a wide variety of gear and transmission applications. Bevel pinion forgings may have several steps that are forged into place based on the finished machining design given by the customer.

    KDK can heat treat the pinion gears as well for increased machinability. Forged gear shafts increase strength of the gear and create less waste of material. 8620 is a common alloy steel material that pinions are forged from.

    There are a variety or gearing profiles that manufacturers use in creating a final machined gear. Pinion gears may have gear profiles on both ends of shaft. Many of KDK's customers have utilized our upset forging expertise in order to save material and increase the strength of their gears and transmissions.
