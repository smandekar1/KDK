    • Product Description


    KDK produces axle forgings that are then machined into finished axles and can be assembled in modular powertrain systems. KDK can produce a forging blank that would leave a minimal amount of cleanup necessary allowing for less machining time. Axles are used in a variety of transportation applications like cars, trucks, and off road vehicles.

    Experience


    KDK has been producing valve stem forgings for about 13 years. KDK is capable of producing valve stem blanks in a variety of different materials based upon customer specification.

    Applications


    KDK's axle components are used in a variety of transportation applications.
