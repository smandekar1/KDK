    • Product Description


    While valve handles may be made from castings, forged valve handles are stronger with improved grain flow from the forging process. Valve handles are used to control flow valves of various kinds. KDK produces other types of handles as well.

    Depending on your requirements, KDK will make a custom die for your handle requirements. Please send us a copy of your drawing and we will be happy to review your part and send a quote to you.

    Applications


    Valve handles are used to control flow of square stem valves. KDK produces many different forgings for a variety of different market segments. For more information about the parts we produce, please click on the products tab.
