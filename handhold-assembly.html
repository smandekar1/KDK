    • Product Description


    Handhold assemblies are configured in many different ways. Each assembly is designed to match the individual passenger or freight car. Attention is given to the material finish of the steel as that can affect the functionality and aesthetics of the product. The holes of the handhold are drilled to create an accurate diameter and the handhold may be screwed or riveted within the car itself. The middle brace section of the assembly is screwed into place.

    Depending on your requirements, KDK will make a custom die for your handhold requirements. Please send us a copy of your drawing and we will be happy to review your part and send a quote to you.

    Experience


    KDK Forging Company has been producing grab irons and handholds for over 40 years. KDK produces these parts in many types of materials including carbon steel. KDK supplies handholds to top tier companies that manufacturing passenger and freight rail cars.

    Applications


    Handholds and handhold assemblies are used in the passenger and freight rail industry. KDK produces many different forgings for a variety of different market segments. For more information about the parts we produce, please click on the products tab.
